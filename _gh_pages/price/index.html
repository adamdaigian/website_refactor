Pricing

Appedo licensing is very simple.

You get a free lite version of Appedo forever. This will have a data retention of last 1 hour.

Appedo server monitoring and deep-dive diagnostics are licensed per agent.

Appedo load testing engine is licensed based on virtual user hours. (VUH).

Appedo end user monitoring is licensed based on number of measurements. Basic unit of measurement is one page view from one location.

For your needs, please send a mail to sales@appedo.com
